Welcome to Dexter Dexter formulations private limited, established in 2009, is on its path to become one of the leading manufactures in the medical industry, which produces both surgical products and allopathic medicine. Dexter manufactures all products using first grade raw material and adhering to international process and standards. The company focuses on providing high quality, appropriately priced products to its customers and supports all these with dedicated customer service.

Dexter has a good network with national and international traders and distributors. This aspect ensures availability of dexter S products in the global market. Team dexter focuses on social responsibility through a sophisticated treatment using latest technology. It aims to create awareness among the public about medical hygiene. Dexter is prompt and perfect. It always moves with time management.
